Projects

Fall-Safe

As my final year engineering capstone project I, along with 3 of my colleagues, designed and created a home analytics/monitoring device. This device, Fall-Safe, is meant to be worn at the waist by elderly people who have a high risk of falls or if a medical professional would like to obtain information about their day-to-day movement.

Fall-Safe when worn will collect information regarding the users speed and mobility and relay that via bluetooth to a nearby basestation. The base station will use a machine learning algorithm to compute the users current state of movement and detect any falls. All this information can be accessed remotely by a concerned third-party (loved one or health-care professional) through the Fall-Safe web app, in a user friendly and easy to understand format. In the event of a fall an alert will pop up on this app alerting those appropriate.
